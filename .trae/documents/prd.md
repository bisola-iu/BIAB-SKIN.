# BIAB Skincare Website - Product Requirements Document

## 1. Product Overview
A beautiful, feminine e-commerce website for BIAB, a luxury skincare brand with a welcoming and soft aesthetic.

### 1.1 Purpose
- Showcase BIAB skincare products with an elegant design
- Provide intuitive product discovery and shopping experience
- Convey brand story and values
- Enable complete shopping flow from browsing to checkout

### 1.2 Target Audience
- Women aged 25-45 seeking luxury skincare products
- Customers who value natural, effective skincare
- Shoppers looking for a premium shopping experience

## 2. Features

### 2.1 Homepage
- Hero section with brand tagline
- Brand introduction with imagery
- Featured products carousel
- Quick navigation to main sections

### 2.2 Product Catalog
- Grid/List view of all products
- Product cards with images, descriptions, prices
- Add-to-cart functionality
- Product filtering

### 2.3 Product Detail Pages
- Detailed product descriptions
- Ingredients list
- Usage instructions
- Customer reviews section
- Add-to-cart and wishlist options

### 2.4 About Section
- Brand story
- Mission and values
- Team introduction

### 2.5 Contact Section
- Contact form
- Customer service information
- FAQ section

### 2.6 Shopping Cart & Checkout
- Cart summary page
- Checkout flow overview
- Payment information form

### 2.7 Footer
- Navigation links
- Social media links
- Newsletter sign-up
- Copyright information

## 3. Design Requirements

### 3.1 Color Palette
- **Primary Colors**: Baby pink (#F8C8DC), Soft pink (#FADADD)
- **Accents**: Light coral (#FFB6C1), Dusty rose (#E8B4B8)
- **Neutrals**: Cream (#FFF9F9), Warm white (#FFFEFE)

### 3.2 Typography
- **Display Font**: Elegant serif (Playfair Display or similar)
- **Body Font**: Clean sans-serif (Lato or similar)
- **Hierarchy**: Clear H1-H6 with generous line spacing

### 3.3 Visual Style
- Soft, feminine aesthetic
- Welcoming and luxurious feel
- Minimal but thoughtful animations
- High-quality product imagery
- Clean, well-spaced layouts

### 3.4 User Experience
- Intuitive navigation
- Smooth transitions
- Responsive design for all devices
- Accessible for all users
- Encourages browsing and purchasing

## 4. Technical Requirements

- **Technology Stack**: Pure HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first, fully responsive
- **Animations**: CSS transitions and animations
- **Performance**: Optimized images and code
- **Browser Support**: Modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
